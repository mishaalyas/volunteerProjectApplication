  • What is Volunteering By CUNY Students?

    It is a platform for Participating CUNY Colleges' students to track their volunteering hours.

    Why is Volunteering By CUNY Students Useful?

    Volunteering is very important for personal and professional development. However, tracking hours and then reporting hours may require a lot of time. This application can help students track their hours and fill out their nonprofits information.

    Can ANY CUNY Student use this?

    Our goal is that every CUNY College uses this. However, the CUNY College must be a participating college, where the Community Service Department of the College helps students become more aware of the volunteering opportunities near their college.

    Where can I Volunteer?

    There are many nonprofits in NYC that can use your help. Learn more about the nonprofits in NYC
